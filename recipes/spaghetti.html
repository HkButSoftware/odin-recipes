Spaghetti Recipe

Traditional no fuss Spaghetti

This spaghetti is the best no fuss no muss no nonsense spaghetti you can make!

Perfect for struggling students wanting to make a tasty easy to make everyday dish, not requiring a lot of effort and easy for beginners !

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Half an onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Black Pepper (Optional)
  • Cajun Spice (Optional)
  • Paprika (Optional)

Steps

  1. Fill your boiling bowl with water till quarter to top
  2. Pour quarter a cup of oil
  3. One spoon of salt, leave to boil
  4. While the water is boiling, chop your onion in half and wash (other half can be kept in fridge for a day or two max)
  5. If you have an onion cutter, cut till you have small cubes of onions, otherwise handcut till it's all small cubes
  6. After water starts boiling, put half/one third of a bag of spaghetti(Depends how much you want)
  7. leave for a minute till the spaghetti falls into bowl, make sure to keep mixing the spaghetti a little bit
  8. Once the spaghetti has a ligher color closer to white, filter out the water and keep the spaghetti in a different bowl
  9. Using the bowl you boiled the spaghetti with, put quarter of a cup of oil
  10. Pour onions into the the bowl
  11. Make sure to keep turning the onions inside the bowl to not make one side burned
  12. Once the onions are reddened, Pour half of a Spaghetti sauce cup inside
  13. Make sure to turn it around a little so that all onions are submerged
  14. Proceed to put all spices you prefer except chili flakes, no specifc order is needed, preferably half or one spoon of each
  15. Put the spaghetti inside the bowl and mix it all
  16. Once the spaghetti is fully covered in sauce, leave for a minute or two, then pour into serving plate
  17. Garnish with chili flakes on top, not too much though just enough to give it a lil kick
  18. Say Bismillah, and enjoy
