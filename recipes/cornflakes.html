This is the way i make my cornflakes for all the haters out there

Everybody hates on the way i make my cornflakes, but the erason is they can't imitate my flawless beautiful work!

Remember, don't knock it till you try it!

Ingredients

  • Powdered milk
  • Sugar
  • Cornflakes (obv)
  • Water Boiler (the device not on heat)

Steps

  1. Boil water using the water boiler
  2. In your serving bowl, pour 2 spoonfuls of powdered milk
  3. Take your water and pour into the bowl, mix thoroughly
  4. Put desired amount of sugar, preferably 2 spoons (i got a sweet tooth)
  5. Mix thoroughly
  6. Pour Cornflakes, if you want it to be crisper don't mix too much, if not mix thoroughly
  7. Say Bismillah, and enjoy!
