Chicken Sandwhich as a snack (or dinner if you're broke)

This beautiful spicy chicken sandwhich can serve as a great snack for a movie night, gamenight, or as a broke college students dinner!

Ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • butter
  • 1 pound, skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • cup hot sauce
  • 4 (10 inch) tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded lettuce
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • Ketchup (Optional)
  • Mayonnaise (Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat until butter melts
  2. Add chicken
  3. cook and stir until no longer pink in middle and juices run clear
  4. after about 10 minutes, remove from heat
  5. Pour hot sauce into the skillet until chicken is coated
  6. Lay tortillas and pour a spoonful of ketchup and Mayonnaise, mix thoroughly
  7. Distribute chicken evenly
  8. Top the chicken with lettuce and celery
  9. Fold tortillas, and cut in half if preferred
  10. Say Bismillah, and enjoy!
